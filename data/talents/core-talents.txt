# Acrobat
Type: Core
Requirements: 3 AGI
Effect: You upgrade all Reflex tests you attempt.

# Actor
Type: Core
Requirements: 3 GUI
Effect: You upgrade all Deception tests you attempt.

# Advanced Study (A)
Type: Core
Requirements: Level 10, must be a spellcaster
Effect: You learn 1 advanced spell chosen from your class' spell list. If you are an Ix or Medusa, have the Sorcerous talent, or have the Occultist background, you may take this talent to add a spell to your natural spellcasting ability or warlock pact. You may take this talent multiple times. You must choose a new spell if you do so.

# Against the Empyrean
Type: Core
Requirements: You may not be a spellcaster
Effect: You resist all damage dealt to you by spells. You upgrade any non-area attack tests you attempt which targets a Demon, Spirit or spellcaster.

# All Out (W)
Type: Core
Requirements: 4 PER, 4 STR
Effect: When you take the Attack [A], you may use your [R] to upgrade each attack test you attempt as part of the action once.

# Angler (W)

# Apothecary

# Arcane Duellist (A)

# Authoritative Source
Type: Core
Requirements: 4 GUI, you must not be a peasant, prole, or general dirt-farmer type
Effect: Peasants, proles, and general dirt-farmer types believe what you say unless evidence to the contrary is presented. Note that evidence might range from a more-trusted community leader (such as a priest or lord) or from definitive personal experience (you can't convince someone who sees colour that the sky isn't blue just by saying so).

# Grappler (W)
Type: Core
Requirements: 4 STR
Effect: You upgrade all Melee tests you attempt to Grapple an enemy, and all Fitness or Reflex tests you attempt to escape a Grapple. When an enemy Grapples you, you may take 1 Friction to immediately attempt to Escape from the Grapple.

# Gunslinger (W)
Type: Core
Requirements: 4 PER
Effect: You may treat lever carbines and lever rifles you wield as having the Auto property. You may also do so with light revolvers and revolvers you wield, but only if you have a free hand. For any of these weapons, you do not need to use any action to swap the weapon between Auto and "normal" firing modes. Attacks that benefit from this ability can only be made at short range. When you drop an item, you may use your [I] to draw a one-handed ranged weapon. You can reload a one-handed ranged weapon even if you don't have a free hand.

# Heaven and Earth (W)

Multiattack: When you take the Attack action, you make two attacks, rather than one. These attacks may be made with the same weapon or different ones (if you are wielding multiple weapons) and can target the same or different targets.

# Twinned Spell (A)
Type: Core
Requirements: Must be a spellcaster
Effect: When you succeed a spellcasting test, you may take 1 Fatigue to copy that spell before you activate any boosts. You choose a new target(s) for the copy, be it an Actor, Object, or area. Both spells are resolved at the same time and they cannot affect the same targets, even after activating boosts which allow them to affect additional targets. If the spells affect an area, the copy's area cannot overlap with the original's. You activate boosts separately for both the original and copy spell, and they both draw from the same pool of Advantages.
