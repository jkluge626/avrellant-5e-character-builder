# Ablation
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: When you use your Gadget to grant a creature DR, you increase the DR granted by 1.

# Aetherproofing
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: When you use your Gadget to grant a creature Resistance to a damage type, they also gain Resistance to Aether damage.

# Arcane Development
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: Level 9
Effect: You gain an Arcane (A) talent. You must meet its prerequisites.

# Armourer
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: You increase the DR of any armour you wear by 1.

# Autodidact
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: Level 6
Effect: You gain a Core Talent. You must meet its prerequisites.

# Battery Detonation
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: When you use your Gadget to deal damage, you may take 1 Strain to increase the damage by 1d6.

# Be Prepared
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: Whenever you finish a Short Rest, you may change your prepared Gadget to a different one you know.

# Blast Proof
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: You have Resistance to damage dealt by your own Gadgets and by area effects.

# Calculated
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: When you roll Initiative, if you roll lower than your Intellect, you may use your Intellect instead.

# Cannibalize
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: When you use your Maintenance ability to repair your Gadget, you regain 2 Gadget charges rather than 1.

# Capacitor
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: Your Gadget charge maximum increases by 2.

# Careful Preparation
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: When you prepare a Gadget during a Short Rest, you may prepare two Gadgets instead of one. You may only have one prepared at a time, but you may switch between your two prepared Gadgets as an Instant on your turn.

# Combat Tinkerer
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: You may use your Maintenance ability as an Instant on your turn, rather than as an Action.

# Contingency
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: Level 6
Effect: When you are reduced to 0 Wounds, your Gadget may immediately activate as a Reaction, even if you are Incapacitated. This activation does not require a Gadget charge.

# Controlled Detonation
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: When you use a Gadget that deals damage in an area, you may choose a number of creatures equal to your Intellect Bonus. Those creatures automatically succeed on any test to avoid or reduce the damage.

# Dual Wielder
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: You may have two Gadgets prepared at once. When you activate a Gadget, you choose which of your prepared Gadgets to activate.

# Efficient Design
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: When you activate your Gadget, you may take 1 Stress to activate it without spending a Gadget charge.

# Emergency Activation
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: You may use your Reaction to activate your Gadget when you or an ally within 10m of you takes damage.

# Emergency Repairs
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: When you use your Maintenance ability, you may take 1 Strain to regain 3 Gadget charges rather than 1.

# Enhanced Gadget
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: When you activate your Gadget, you may take 1 Strain to enhance its effects. The specific enhancement depends on the Gadget type (increased range, duration, damage, healing, etc.).

# Experimental Design
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: Level 6
Effect: You learn an additional Gadget specialization. You may prepare Gadgets from this specialization.

# Expert Tinkerer
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: You upgrade all Tinker tests you attempt.

# Fortified Position
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: When you use your Gadget to create an object or structure, it has double the normal Wounds and DR.

# Grenadier
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: Once on your turn, you may take 1 Stress to throw a grenade without using your Action.

# Hair Trigger
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: When you roll Initiative, your Gadget automatically activates. This activation does not require a Gadget charge or an Action.

# Hardened Gadget
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: Your Gadget cannot be damaged or destroyed by attacks or environmental effects.

# Improved Efficiency
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: Once per turn, when you activate your Gadget, you do not expend a charge.

# Infusion
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: When you activate your Gadget on an ally, you may grant them one of the following benefits for 1 minute: +1 to attack rolls, +1 to damage rolls, +1 to DR, or +2m Movement.

# Innovation
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: Level 9
Effect: You invent a unique Gadget with effects you design. Work with your DM to create a balanced Gadget appropriate to your level.

# Insulated
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: You have Resistance to Lightning damage.

# Jury Rig
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: You may use your Maintenance ability on any broken object or device, not just your Gadget. You restore the item to working condition.

# Lasting Effect
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: When you use your Gadget to apply a beneficial effect with a duration, that duration is doubled.

# Miniaturization
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: Your Gadgets are half their normal size and weight. They are easier to conceal and transport.

# Modular Design
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: Level 6
Effect: You may change your prepared Gadget as an Action, rather than only during a Short Rest.

# Overcharge
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: When you activate your Gadget, you may spend 2 charges instead of 1 to double one numerical effect (damage, healing, range, duration, etc.).

# Personal Shield
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: When you activate your Gadget on yourself, you gain 2 DR until the start of your next turn.

# Power Surge
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: Level 6
Effect: Once per Short Rest, when you activate your Gadget, you may activate it without spending a charge and with enhanced effects (as if you used Enhanced Gadget).

# Precision Tools
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: You upgrade all Tinker tests you attempt to repair, modify, or create mechanical devices.

# Quick Deploy
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: You may activate your Gadget as an Instant on your turn, rather than as an Action.

# Redundant Systems
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: When your Gadget would be destroyed or broken, it instead becomes inert until you use your Maintenance ability to repair it.

# Reinforced Construction
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: Objects and structures created by your Gadget have +5 Wounds.

# Remote Activation
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: You may activate your Gadget on a target within 20m of you, rather than within 10m.

# Resourceful
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: When you finish a Short Rest, you regain all spent Gadget charges rather than half.

# Retrofit
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: You may spend 10 minutes to modify a weapon or piece of armour. The item gains +1 to attack rolls, damage rolls, or DR (your choice) for 24 hours.

# Rugged Construction
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: Your Gadget has Wounds equal to 5 times your level and DR equal to your level.

# Scavenger
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: You upgrade all tests you attempt to find crafting materials or salvage components from broken devices.

# Shielded
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: While your Gadget is active, you have Resistance to one damage type of your choice.

# Spell Battery
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: You may use your Gadget charges to cast spells you know. Each spell costs a number of charges equal to its Instability.

# Stabilize
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: When you use your Gadget to grant a beneficial effect, the target also removes one Condition affecting them.

# Streamlined Design
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: Your Gadget weighs half as much and can be deployed or stowed as an Instant.

# Surge Protector
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: You and allies within 5m of you have Resistance to Lightning damage.

# Timed Detonation
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: When you activate your Gadget, you may delay its activation by up to 1 minute. You choose the exact timing of the delayed activation.

# Tinkerer's Eye
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: You may spend 1 minute examining a mechanical device to learn its function, how to operate it, and any hidden features or traps.

# Weapon Integration
Type: Class
Class: Artificer
Requirements: None
Effect: You integrate a one-handed weapon into your Gadget. You may make attacks with this weapon as part of activating your Gadget.
