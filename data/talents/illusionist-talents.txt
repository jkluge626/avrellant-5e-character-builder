# Arcane Development
Type: Class
Class: Illusionist
Requirements: Level 9
Effect: You gain an arcane (A) talent. You must meet the prerequisites for the talent.

# Autodidact
Type: Class
Class: Illusionist
Requirements: None
Effect: You gain 2 Skill Points. You may take this talent multiple times.

# Avoidance
Type: Class
Class: Illusionist
Requirements: Level 6
Effect: While you are wearing padded, light, or no armour at all, you increase your Evasion by 2.

# Awestruck
Type: Class
Class: Illusionist
Requirements: Level 6
Effect: After you use your Captive Audience ability, you degrade all attack tests attempted against you by the enemies which provided modifiers to you. This effect lasts until the start of your next turn. This ability is an arcane ability.

# Balanced Landing
Type: Class
Class: Illusionist
Requirements: None
Effect: You halve any Wounds and Cripple stacks you take from falling. Wounds you take from falling cannot Incapacitate you.

# Be Prepared
Type: Class
Class: Illusionist
Requirements: None
Effect: You ignore the negative effects of rolling doubles or triples (but not miscasts) when resolving an unfettered Spellcasting test for your prepared spells.

# Boil Over
Type: Class
Class: Illusionist
Requirements: Level 6
Effect: Once during a round of combat, you may spend Inspiration to regain your [R].

# Careful
Type: Class
Class: Illusionist
Requirements: None

# Catfall
Type: Class

# Charlatan

# Charming

# Constitution

# Craft
Type: Class

# Dead Hard
Type: Class
Class: Illusionist
Requirements: None
Effect: When you push yourself, you may immediately move up to Xm, where X is your Agility. This movement counts as Disengaging.

# Decisive Strike
Type: Class
Class: Illusionist
Requirements: None
Effect: When an enemy Grapples you, you may take 1 Friction to immediately attempt to Escape the Grapple.

# Devour Hope
Type: Class
Class: Illusionist
Requirements: Level 11
Effect: When one of your Illusionist spells or abilities (except your Smoke and Mirrors movement) causes an enemy to become unaware of you, you inflict stacks of Fear equal to half your Guile on that enemy.

# Dreadlord
Type: Class
Class: Illusionist
Requirements: None
Effect: When you use your [A] to make an attack, you may take 1 Stress to cast Terrify or its Eye of Fear morph as an Illusionist spell. You must do so either immediately before or immediately after the attack resolves.

# Dodge
Type: Class
Class: Illusionist
Requirements: None
Effect: [R] when you are hit by an attack. You may negate that hit.

# Driven
Type: Class

# Educated

# Eleventh Hour

# Escapist
Type: Class
Class: Illusionist
Requirements: None

# Evasive

# Everyman
Type: Class
Class: Illusionist
Requirements: None
Effect: Craftsmen, farmers, labourers, and woodsmen are positively inclined towards you and don't consider your presence a threat unless you really look out of place or are somewhere you're very obviously not supposed to be.

# Expert
Type: Class
Class: Illusionist
Requirements: None
Effect: You choose an additional Skill to benefit from your Expertise ability. At 6th level, you may take this talent twice (in total), increasing to thrice at 10th level. You must pick a different Skill each time you take this talent.

# Game Recognise Game
Type: Class
Class: Illusionist
Requirements: Level 14
Effect: You recognise disguising illusions (such as those with the Disguise spell) you can see within 5m and can tell when a shapeshifting Actor you can see within 5m is disguised as something they are not. You do not learn the true nature of the observed Actor, only that they are not what they appear to be.

# Give the Floor
Type: Class
Class: Illusionist
Requirements: None
Effect: When you cast Silence as an Illusionist spell, you can exempt any Actor from being affected by it while the spell is in effect. You may exempt or un-exempt an Actor as an [I].

# Horseshoe Effect
Type: Class
Class: Illusionist
Requirements: None
Effect: Guards, mercenaries, militiamen, police, and soldiers are positively inclined towards you and don't consider your presence a threat unless you really look out of place or are somewhere you're very obviously not supposed to be.

# Kick
Type: Class
Class: Illusionist
Requirements: None
Effect: Once on your turn, you may take 1 Stress to kick a Size-4 or smaller Actor within reach. The target takes Force damage equal to your Strength. Regardless of if the target is Wounded or not by the kick, it must attempt Composure tests to maintain concentration on any spells it is concentrating on, as if the kick had Wounded it.

# Mass Hysteria

# Misdirection
