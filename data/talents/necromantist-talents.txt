# Vigil
Type: Class
Class: Necromantist
Requirements: Level 17
Effect: If an ally within 2m of you takes Wounds in excess of their Wound Threshold, they do not immediately die. Instead, they are Incapacitated (and must remain so while their Wounds are above their threshold). If at any point their Wounds exceed twice their Wound Threshold, they die. You must not be Incapacitated yourself for this ability to activate. If, at the end of your next turn, the allies' Wounds still exceed their Wound Threshold and you are not within 2m of the affected ally or are yourself Incapacitated, the affected ally dies.
