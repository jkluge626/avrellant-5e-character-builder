# Adrenaline Rush
Type: Class
Class: Armsman
Requirements: None
Effect: You gain 1 Adrenaline Token when you push yourself.

# Alert
Type: Class
Class: Armsman
Requirements: Level 9
Effect: You may take Reactions and use your activated abilities against enemies which you are otherwise unaware of.

# Armourer
Type: Class
Class: Armsman
Requirements: None
Effect: You increase the DR of any armour you wear by 1.

# Beefy
Type: Class
Class: Armsman
Requirements: None
Effect: Your Pain Suppression ability decreases damage by a further X, where X is your Size.

# Bloodless
Type: Class
Class: Armsman
Requirements: None
Effect: You halve the number of Wounds that stacks of Bleed inflict on you.

# Bodyguard
Type: Class
Class: Armsman
Requirements: Level 6
Effect: When an ally within 1m of you is hit by a ranged attack, you may use your Reaction to instead apply the hit against yourself.

# Brawler
Type: Class
Class: Armsman
Requirements: None
Effect: You upgrade all Skill tests you attempt to Grapple a target or to Escape a Grapple.

# Cautious
Type: Class
Class: Armsman
Requirements: None
Effect: When you take Strain to mitigate damage, you reduce the damage by an additional 1 per Strain tally taken.

# Combat Training
Type: Class
Class: Armsman
Requirements: Level 9
Effect: You gain a weapon (W) talent. You must meet the prerequisites for the talent.

# Deft Hands
Type: Class
Class: Armsman
Requirements: None
Effect: On your turn, you may take 1 Stress to take the Reload action as an Instant. You may only do so any time you could take an Action.

# Determination
Type: Class
Class: Armsman
Requirements: None
Effect: You gain Inspiration when you use your Second Wind ability.

# Driven
Type: Class
Class: Armsman
Requirements: None
Effect: You may keep two instances of Inspiration, rather than just one.

# Fighter
Type: Class
Class: Armsman
Requirements: None
Effect: You gain an additional style from your Fighting Style ability.

# Grenadier
Type: Class
Class: Armsman
Requirements: None
Effect: Once on your turn, you may take 1 Stress to throw a grenade without using your Action.

# Meathead
Type: Class
Class: Armsman
Requirements: None
Effect: When you attempt a Fitness test, you may spend 1 Adrenaline token to upgrade that test. Your Wound threshold increases by 1.

# Victory Rush
Type: Class
Class: Armsman
Requirements: None
Effect: When you kill or Incapacitate an enemy with an attack, you gain 1 Adrenaline Token. You may not benefit from this ability again until the start of your next turn.

# Warfighter
Type: Class
Class: Armsman
Requirements: None
Effect: You increase the number of Adrenaline Tokens you generate at the start of combat as well as your token maximum by 1 each.
